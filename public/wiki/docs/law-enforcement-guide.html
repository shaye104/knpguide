*Only does the following rule apply to crimes committed against one victim. For example, murder of Person A and aggravated assault of Person B is a lawful reason to stack 
two (2) charges without double jeopardy. A maximum of two (2) charges shall be allowed to be placed as arrest reasons against a perpetrator no matter the amount of 
victims and infractions. In such the most severe charges such as crimes against life, liberty and health take preference per recommendation.  
*Mass Murder may never receive additional battery (assault) charges despite the number of victims.
